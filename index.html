Sleepy Heads

Designed to assist individual in finding holistic sleep remedies all in one place. Your Personal Holistic Sleep resource for Better Nights and Brighter Days.

Duration: 2 Weeks

Key Skills: Interviews, Ideation, User Persona, Wireframing, Affinity Diagram, Story Boarding, Visual Design, Competitor Analysis

Motivation

Sleep is arguably the most important aspects to overall health. Sleepy Heads gives users a variety of holistic remedies to help assist in obtaining quality sleep.

Context

Problem

Target

Brief

Many individuals struggle with sleep due to stress and anxiety, seek holistic techniques and tools for stress-relief to gain better quality sleep.

Individuals of all ages who are looking for holistic aids to assist in them getting a better quality of sleep amidst their stress.

Sleepy Heads is a collaborative effort to offer holistic stress-relief aids to users, assisting them in relaxation and improve their sleep quality.
